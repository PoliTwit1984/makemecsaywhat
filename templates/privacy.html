Privacy Policy

Overview

At Make MEC Say What?, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Data Collection

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Generated video content
  • Generated audio content
  • Text inputs for video generation

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Location data
  • User accounts or profiles

Data Usage

The data we collect is used solely for:

  • Generating your requested videos
  • Displaying your videos in the gallery
  • Improving our service quality

Data Storage

Generated videos are stored temporarily and may be automatically removed after a period of time. We recommend downloading your videos for long-term storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • ElevenLabs for voice synthesis
  • Hedra for video generation

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. We encourage you to review their policies for more information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your generated content
  • Download your videos
  • Request removal of your content

Contact

For any privacy-related concerns or requests, please contact the site administrator.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Please check back regularly for any changes.
