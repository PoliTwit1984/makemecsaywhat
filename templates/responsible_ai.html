Responsible AI Usage Guidelines

Understanding AI-Generated Content

Make MEC Say What? uses advanced AI technology to generate synthetic videos. While this technology offers creative possibilities, it's important to understand its implications and use it responsibly.

Guidelines for Responsible Use

  • Do not create content that could mislead or deceive others
  • Avoid generating harmful, offensive, or inappropriate content
  • Respect intellectual property rights and copyrights
  • Consider the potential impact of your content on others
  • Be transparent about the AI-generated nature of your content

Content Restrictions

Please refrain from generating:

  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Misleading or false information
  • Content that could cause harm or distress
  • Material that violates privacy or personal rights

Best Practices

  • Always disclose when sharing that the content is AI-generated
  • Consider the context in which your content will be viewed
  • Think about potential misuse or misinterpretation
  • Download and secure your content appropriately

Legal Considerations

Users are responsible for:

  • Ensuring their use complies with applicable laws
  • Obtaining necessary permissions for content use
  • Understanding and following platform terms of service

Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines or have concerns about AI-generated content, please contact the site administrator.

Final Note

Remember: With great technology comes great responsibility. Use this tool thoughtfully and consider the impact of your content on others.
